    What is considered a recognizable person?

    We want to figure out how many people are in each of these photos so that we can later tag them. Use your best judgement in identifying individuals.

    In the context of these photos, a recognizable person:

    1. has the majority of their face/heads in a picture,
    2. does not also include their reflections (only count one person once),
    3. and is a human and not an animal.
